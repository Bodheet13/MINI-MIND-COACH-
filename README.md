# Mini Mind Coach

A supportive mental wellness companion application designed to help users develop positive mental health habits and mindfulness practices.

## Overview

Mini Mind Coach is a lightweight application aimed at providing accessible mental wellness support through:

- **Daily Check-ins**: Track your mood and emotional state
- **Mindfulness Exercises**: Simple breathing and relaxation techniques
- **Positive Affirmations**: Encouraging messages to boost mental wellbeing
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your wellness journey over time
